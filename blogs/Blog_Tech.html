The technology of the future

December 1, 2021

I'm back with another blog to talk about the possible technological progress and the possibility of it completely changing the world and the life of humans.

There is no denying the rapid changes in our world and environment. Humans have enhanced the technology of our time in such a short period. Remember when we used to have huge box size televisions and slow home computers? Those felt like ages ago, but it is only because of the fast development of our technology that most inventions were already left behind. And the transition will simply keep going quicker as time goes by.

But how will the world look in the next 10 to 20 years? That we don't have the answer of as of yet. However, we may have seen a glimpse of what our technology has destined for us.

Artificial Intelligence

As you may have been aware, AI's or Artificial Intelligence is the human-designed machine that can simulate the mind of a human. The concept is still relatively new, and only a few creations have been finished. But the potential of AI to completely copy a human mind can be beneficial for us, or it could also be detrimental to our existence.

The crucial question would be, can AI and humans live together in the future? We know AI has been a common theme in some films in general. But we could be closer to the harsh truth of the chances that AI could replace humans. If in a dystopian future, we become careless with our actions that cause doom.

Overall, there is this fear right now with most people when asked about the possibility of AI replacing us. Could this be another social issue that we have to fix in the future? Will AI have the same rights and privileges as humans? Will they be more superior than us? We could have the answer to this sooner than we'd think with the progress of our technology.

The future of Earth

Our future doesn't revolve around the idea of AI solely. There are other paths that our technology could progress. The concepts of virtual spaces are there, and we might be able to interact in the future through a new era of computing. As technology advances, we might be able to create a technology to cure cancer, save more lives, reverse climate change, and overall improve the lives of every human being, including its environment. Only time will tell how our destiny will unfold.

That's everything for now. Thank you once again for taking the time to read my blogs about topics that are relevant to our time. Stay updated with my technology blogs by checking my website for content. Stay safe and techie everyone!
