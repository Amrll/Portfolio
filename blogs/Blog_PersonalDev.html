My growth as a person and the different paths I took led me to my computer science passion.

November 18,2021

Let's delve into my decisions in life and how they led me to my position now. And have an insight into how I became passionate about computers and my ambition to be a game developer.

When we were children, we'd always dream about our dreams in life and how we would achieve them. I remember my dream as a kid was to be a scientist or an astronaut. And I would travel into space and find new life on other planets. But as I got older, I was introduced to computers. I was amazed at what computers can do. And since then, my dream is to be able to program and create my own game.

Video games like Grand theft Auto, Fallout, Call of duty, Minecraft, and Pokemon, sparked the passion in me to become a game developer.

Due to my passion, I took a Computer Science course in my college.

However, before I reach college, I face a lot of ups and downs. It was junior high when I almost got dropped out of school due to being absent for three months. I had no reason for that, but I was burnt out, and I lost interest in studying.

Luckily I rediscovered my passion, and I decided that it was time to fix myself when I reached Senior High.

Now I have the ambition. All it takes for me is to continue doing my best to study. So far, it hasn't been easy for me because I have a lot of catching up to do compared to some of my computer science classmates. I am entirely new to programming.

But I am never giving up my dream to become a game developer. My passion and heart are focused on one thing, and that is to be able to succeed in this path that I chose.
