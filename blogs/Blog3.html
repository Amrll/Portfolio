Blog #3

September 10, 2021

It's my third blog, and so far, my online classes has been stressful and fun at the same time. And for Pc Troubleshooting, things are really going so fast, we're given so many information that I am barely keeping up, I hope that I can do my best.

For our module 2, Our teacher discusses to us about the parts of computers. Some of the topics were about the hardware and software components of the computer, the input and output devices, and all the internal and external parts of computer.

Overall, it was an easy and refreshing lesson because I know most of it already. It is still great to listen, because it further improves my knowledge about computers.

Now, I was given some questions that I am going to answer with you guys.

A. If you will become a computer parts/gadgets or any ICT tools, what will you be and why?


If I am going to choose a Computer component that best describe me, it would be the Hard Disk Drive or HDD. It is for the fact that it is the component that I can associate my personality as a student. I love storing information inside my brain, no matter what knowledge it may be. I have grown an appreciation in upgrading myself, maybe back then, my brain would just be in 1 terabyte space, but now, I have done my best to maximize my potential into the range of petabyte or even more. Although I may not be as quick and fast as the Solid State Drive (SSD), I am taking my time and not rushing myself in learning and storing information, because I know genuinely that I am more durable in the long run.

B. What is the use of computer in your day to day activity?

There is not a single day that I don't apply or utilize computer in my life. I am probably in front of computer too much for my own good. But what type of purpose does it serve? I play videogames with it, do my activities and school works, I use it to socialize with friends, and countless more reasons and purpose. Overall, it has done me a favor of being there always whenever I need a technology that can ease my tasks.

That is all for my blog this time, I hope that you guys have a great weekend and to always stay safe. Perhaps computer can help us in some way in figuring out how to cure Covid-19, but until then, Wear your masks and practice social distancing.
