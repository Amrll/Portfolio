My favorite sports

November 27, 2021

Take a look at the sports I play and how they helped improve my physical lifestyle.

Basketball

I love playing basketball. I was invited by my friends back in elementary to play ball, and ever since then, the game has been one of my favorites. I follow famous athletes like Lebron James, Stephen Curry, and Kobe Bryant. But the athlete that inspired me even further is Dwyane Wade. The guy was one of the best when he played. His ability to be clutch and deliver big shots when his team needed him the most is one of his best assets. His posters are incredible. I remember him dunking over Varejao and Perkins and being blown out of my mind the way he posterized those guys.

Cycling/Biking

Cycling is a sport that I recently found beneficial to my health. I have known biking for a while now. But I had never thought about it as a sport and in a serious manner. Then during the quarantine, I had my first climb in the mountain to Morong, Bataan. I was genuinely happy because it was so fun to experience new things in life. My health was not the best before I did cycling, and I knew I had to change something, and I did!

That's it for now. I hope that I inspired you to also go out there and explore the world and discover hobbies that you'll love. I appreciate your time for reading another blog from me. Stay tune for more upcoming blogs about topics such as technology, social media, or my online classes.
