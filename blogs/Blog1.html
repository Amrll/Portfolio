My First Online Class Experience in PC Troubleshooting with Basic Electronics

August 27, 2021

A fun and exciting introduction to my classmates and to the history of different computers and technologies

Our first online class started with our short self introduction, I was so delighted to know the names and different hobbies of my classmates. All of my friends are in different courses so I am a bit nervous, but I hope that I will be able to get along with everyone in BSCS-1A.

How I introduced myself in class:

"Hello, my name po is Reyjohn Ebe, I am 18 year-old. Graduate po ako ng ICT strand from ST. Benilde Center for Global Competence INC."

A short but concise basic information about me, I kept it short so as to not tell anything stupid if I continue saying something.

"Information Overload - Teacher."

is the line that our instructor like to say to us in our first day. I can't blame her though, because she bombarded us with information about the history of computers and how it became what it is today. Though, I enjoyed all the information, and I am thrilled to learn more about this subject.

I am going to be sharing some of my favorites among the technologies that our teacher discussed in a different post soon.
